About Us

Hangover Factory is a punk rock band composed of four dudes who live in Minneapolis, MN. The project started circa 2017 with songs that just about anyone would describe as basic pop punk, but the sound changed over time as the members became more and more frustrated by things occurring in society. Basically, it was increasingly difficult to sing catchy tunes about the banality of inter personal relationships while cops were literally slaying our neighbors and getting away with it. So the song writing started to focus on more important things. If you're into that... Cool. You may like what we do.
